Database Triggers Information

Triggers automatically execute when specific events occur in the database. Current triggers:

  • calculate_patient_payable: Auto-calculates patient payable amount and updates payment status
  • update_bill_status: Updates bill status when payments are made
  • update_bill_status_on_update: Updates status when payments are modified
  • prevent_overpayment: Prevents payments exceeding remaining balance
